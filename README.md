1. What is Machine Learning? 
Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that allows computers to learn 
from data and improve their performance automatically without being explicitly 
programmed. 
In simple terms, instead of writing exact rules for a computer, we give data and let the 
system learn patterns from it. 
Simple Definition 
Machine Learning is the process of training a computer system using data so that it 
can make predictions or decisions automatically. 
Example 
Consider an Email Spam Filter. 
1. The system is given thousands of emails labeled as Spam or Not Spam. 
2. The ML algorithm studies patterns like: 
a. Certain keywords 
b. Sender information 
c. Email structure 
3. After learning these patterns, the system can automatically classify new emails 
as Spam or Not Spam. 
So the computer learns from previous data instead of being manually programmed.

1. Healthy Breakfast Recipe: Vegetable Oats 
Ingredients: 
• 1 cup oats 
• 1 cup water or milk 
• 1/4 cup chopped carrots 
• 1/4 cup peas 
• 1 small onion (chopped) 
• Salt to taste 
• 1 teaspoon oil 
Preparation: 
1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onion. 
2. Add carrots and peas and cook for 2–3 minutes. 
3. Add oats and water (or milk). 
4. Add salt and cook for about 5 minutes until the oats become soft. 
5. Serve hot. 
Benefit: 
This breakfast is rich in fiber, vitamins, and energy, which helps you stay active throughout
